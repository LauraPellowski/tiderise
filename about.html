About Us

At Tiderise Capital, we believe every property has potential—and every community deserves growth. Based in Connecticut, our mission is simple: to transform distressed houses into beautiful, functional homes that strengthen neighborhoods and create opportunities for everyone involved.



Who We Are

Tiderise Capital is a family-driven real estate investment company specializing in fix-and-flip projects, property revitalization, and community renewal. We purchase properties in need of repair, apply thoughtful renovations, and bring them back to life—turning challenges into lasting value.



What We Do

  • For Sellers: We offer fast, fair, and hassle-free solutions for homeowners who would like to sell quickly, even in difficult situations like probate, foreclosure, or significant repair needs.

  • For Buyers: We deliver move-in-ready homes that blend modern upgrades with lasting quality.

  • For Investors & Partners: We provide opportunities to participate in reliable, impact-driven real estate projects that generate strong returns while improving local communities.


Our Promise

Integrity and transparency guide everything we do. From the first conversation to the final closing, we focus on clear communication, fair offers, and win-win solutions.

At Tiderise Capital, we're not just fixing houses; we're building trust, restoring neighborhoods, and helping families find their next home because rising tides raise all boats.
